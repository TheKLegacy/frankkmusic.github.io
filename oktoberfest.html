OKTOBERFST MUSIC
Hire the Frank "K" Duo or Trio for your Oktoberfest!


Oktoberfest Parties are Frank and Fran's specialty. They play all the traditional German songs with Fran adding vocals to include songs in German. They have authentic attire and have continued to add satisfied customers year after year. Fran serenades guests tableside and takes requests making each occasion a memorable one.

The additional benefit to their Oktoberfest performances is that they include all styles/types of music to satisfy the younger crowd as well as all age groups. They entertain for private parties beginning September through November with the band varying in size from two to five musicians.

Frank performed weekly for eight years at the famous Brown Bear, a well-known German restaurant in Chicago, playing for many entertainers brought in from Germany for their Oktoberfest.
